The Spirit of Sacrifice

Eid ul-Adha, the Festival of Sacrifice, commemorates the devotion of Prophet Ibrahim (AS) and his willingness to sacrifice his son Ismail (AS) as an act of obedience to Allah.

This blessed occasion reminds us of the values of faith, sacrifice, and submission to the Divine Will. It teaches us to share our blessings with those in need and to strengthen the bonds of brotherhood.
